About Us

Photo Magic was established in 2021. by a couple of students from Serbia. Out team had a goal of creating high quality yet affordable photo editing service which first started in 2020. on sites like Fiverr and Etsy, but as out popularity has grown we decided to take a risk and start a company and immigrate all of our services there. At first it looked like a failure as we had lost a lot of customers and had to prove ourselves once again, but with a lot of passion, work and support we started to get back on track. At the start of 2022. thanks to each and every customer who chose our service, out company finally became self sustainable and since then it keeps breaking records each month.
